Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What we collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account: email address, username, and profile details. We also collect the fragrance logs, reviews, and scent-of-the-day posts you create within the app.

How we use it

Your data is used to provide and improve the Sillage service — displaying your journal, powering community features, and personalizing your fragrance discovery experience. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL). Photos are stored in Supabase Storage. All connections are encrypted via TLS.

Third-party services

We use Expo for app distribution and push notifications. Push notification tokens are stored to deliver notifications you've opted into. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Your rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at hello@sillageapp.com.

Contact

Questions? Email us at hello@sillageapp.com.
